Frequently asked questions

A few things to know before starting.

Quick answers about DesignLab services, timelines, revisions, and digital projects.

What services can I book?+

DesignLab supports social media graphics, campaign visuals, publication layouts, branding materials, presentations, landing pages, portfolios, forms, trackers, and lightweight workflow systems.

Do you accept both one-off and recurring work?+

Yes. You can book one focused deliverable, a short campaign set, or a recurring design package for ongoing content and promotional needs.

What do you need before a project starts?+

A clear goal, required output sizes, available brand assets, final copy or content, references, deadline, and any technical requirements. DesignLab can help organize incomplete material during discovery.

How are revisions handled?+

Feedback is grouped into clear revision rounds so changes remain organized. The exact number of rounds and project boundaries are confirmed before production begins.

Can DesignLab work with remote clients?+

Yes. Communication, reviews, file delivery, and project tracking can all be handled online through a structured remote workflow.

Do you also build websites and internal tools?+

Yes. DesignLab builds lightweight websites, portfolios, inquiry systems, dashboards, trackers, and Google Apps Script workflows when they support a practical business or content process.
