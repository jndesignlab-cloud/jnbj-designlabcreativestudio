Privacy Policy

Public privacy information for the Planora application by DesignLab Creative Studio.

1. Overview

Planora is a social media planning application by DesignLab Creative Studio. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected when someone uses Planora, why it is used, and how it is handled.

2. Information that may be collected

  • Basic account details such as name, email address, or profile information when a user signs in or creates an account.
  • Content and scheduling information that a user adds inside the app, including captions, notes, post dates, and content planning details.
  • Technical information used for performance, security, and troubleshooting such as browser type, device details, and usage logs.

3. How information is used

Information may be used to provide the service, support account access, organize content planning activity, improve performance, prevent misuse, and communicate service-related updates.

4. Sharing of information

Planora does not sell personal information. Data may only be shared with essential service providers required to operate the application or when disclosure is legally required.

5. Data retention

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to operate the service, fulfill support needs, maintain records, or comply with legal obligations.

6. User choices

Users may request account-related support, ask questions regarding stored information, or request deletion where applicable, subject to technical and legal limitations.

7. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect information, but no platform can guarantee absolute security.

8. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be directed through DesignLab Creative Studio using the website contact form.

9. Updates

This policy may be updated when the application evolves. The latest published version on this page should be treated as the current version.
